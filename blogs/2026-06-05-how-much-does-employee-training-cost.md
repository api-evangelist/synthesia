This comprehensive guide examines employee training expenditures, revealing that U.S. companies spent an average of $874 per learner in 2025. The article breaks down both direct costs (content creation, tools, travel) and indirect costs (employee time, administration, content maintenance), then provides a framework for calculating your organization's specific training costs and identifying opportunities to reduce spending without compromising quality through strategic decisions about content delivery and format.
