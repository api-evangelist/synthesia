Explainer videos are concise promotional videos that explain a product or service to potential customers and can be distributed across websites, social media, YouTube, presentations, webinars, and email campaigns. They typically fall into three categories: animated (2D, 3D, or motion graphics), live-action (actors or talking heads), or screencast demonstrations, and most professional examples combine multiple styles.
