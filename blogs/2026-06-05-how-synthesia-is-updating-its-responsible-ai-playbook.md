Synthesia is transitioning from its original 3Cs framework (consent, control, collaboration) to a new 3Rs framework (Review, Report, React) designed to address modern AI governance challenges. This evolution reflects the company's shift toward risk-based, audit-ready processes and integration of third-party AI models, while maintaining its commitment to enterprise-grade security and responsible AI practices.
