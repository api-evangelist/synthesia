This article examines effective employee onboarding video strategies through ten real-world examples spanning welcome videos, company culture, career development, role-specific training, and compliance content. The piece emphasizes that onboarding is both universal and specific, requiring messages applicable to all new employees while reflecting individual roles and circumstances. The author provides a practical five-step framework using AI video generation along with guidance on measuring success through metrics tied to business impact.
