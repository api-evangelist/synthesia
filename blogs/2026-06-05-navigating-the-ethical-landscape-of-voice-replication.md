This article examines the ethical challenges surrounding voice cloning and AI text-to-speech technology. While these tools offer significant benefits like improved accessibility and personalized communication, they raise critical concerns about consent, privacy, and misuse potential, particularly given documented cases of synthetic voice fraud. The piece emphasizes the necessity of robust regulatory frameworks, transparent practices, and ethical governance to ensure responsible deployment of voice replication technologies.
