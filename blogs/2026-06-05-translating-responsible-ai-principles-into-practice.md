Synthesia has launched an AI Governance Portal to help enterprise customers evaluate the company's approach to responsible AI development. The portal is organized around three categories: Trust and Assurance, Content and Delivery, and Community and Enablement, reflecting the company's broader commitment to embedding governance from the ground up. This initiative addresses growing customer demand for transparency and assurance regarding data handling, content moderation, and vendor risk management.
